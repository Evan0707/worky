  Prisma/PostgreSQL applique RESTRICT par defaut → la suppression d'un User
  sera BLOQUEE si ce user a cree des donnees.

  Relations concernees :
    Photo.createdBy         (schema.prisma:197)
    TimeEntry.createdBy     (schema.prisma:212)
    Material.createdBy      (schema.prisma:227)
    Invoice.createdBy       (schema.prisma:255)
    ProjectNote.createdBy   (schema.prisma:282)
    Task.createdBy          (schema.prisma:335)
    Task.assignee           (schema.prisma:334)
    Message.author          (schema.prisma:368)
    SafetyChecklist.signedBy (schema.prisma:401)

  CORRECTIF : Ajouter onDelete: SetNull a chacune de ces 9 relations.

  5.2. Pas d'endpoint de suppression de compte
